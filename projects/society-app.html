Society Manager

A community app for running a residential society. Members get a directory, notice board, gallery and chat. Committee and admins get a side to add, edit and manage everyone. Front and back, mine.

Role: full stack Flutter Member directory Admin panel Subscriptions

overview

What it does

Society Manager keeps a residential association organised in one place. Members open the app to a home screen with the society details, committee members, the full member directory, a notice board, a photo gallery and chat. Committee members and admins get an extra layer: add and edit members with their roles and grades, manage subscriptions, and post updates. It was built for a real society, so member IDs, grades and roles are part of the model, not an afterthought.

features

The parts that matter

Member directory

Everyone in the society with their role, grade and member ID, searchable.

Notice board, gallery, chat

Announcements, shared photos and a place for members to talk.

Admin side

Add and edit members, set roles and grades, and manage subscriptions.

Committee and about

Committee list and society details, so new members know who runs what.
