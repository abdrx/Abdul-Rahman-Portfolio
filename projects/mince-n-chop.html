project · meat delivery platform

Mince N Chop

An online meat store, end to end. Customers browse, order by the piece or in bulk, pay an advance and track delivery. Behind it runs an operator dashboard and a web storefront. App, admin and web, all mine.

Role: full stack Flutter Admin dashboard Web store Maps Payments

overview

What it does

Mince N Chop is an online meat store for a local butcher. Customers pick from chicken, mutton, beef and fish, order by the piece or place a bulk order with a price per kilo and an advance payment, then follow the store and their delivery on a map. Behind the app sits an operator dashboard for orders, products, customers and earnings, plus a web storefront for anyone who would rather order from a browser. One product, three surfaces.

features

The parts that matter

Shop by category

Chicken, mutton, beef and fish, each with popular cuts and clear pricing.

Bulk orders with advance

Enter weight, get a price per kilo, pay an advance, and see the full bill.

Store and delivery on a map

Find the shop and track delivery, with location baked into every order.

Operator dashboard and web

Manage orders, products and customers from a dashboard, and sell on the web too.
