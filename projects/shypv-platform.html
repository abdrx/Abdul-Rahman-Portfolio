overview

What it is

Shypv (live as Shiper) is a suite of logistics tools that share one account and one look. Instead of stitching together separate apps, a company gets CRM, warehouse management, fleet tracking, transport management, accounting, HR and more on a single platform. I run the ecosystem: 22 connected products, the team building them, the new AI we fold in where it earns its place, and the client work that turns rough requirements into shipped features. Below are three of the products I work across, straight from production.
